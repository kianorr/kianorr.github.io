M-Protect

M-Protect, the virus protector that promised everything and fulfilled nothing, was an overnight sensation. After amassing a cult following of thirteen-year-olds and becoming the sponsor of the school’s crossfire tournament, this antivirus program was seemingly on the up and up. However, its ambition would become its downfall. Getting things done — that’s the M-Protect way This all started when I learned that I could very easily make GUIs with Visual Basic.
